Pipeline

projected light

01
🧠
EEG Capture
Muse 2 headband streams 4-channel EEG at 256 Hz via Bluetooth to BlueMuse, then LSL.

02
⚡
Signal Processing

🤖

Physical Setup

Projected onto a
dress-form mannequin

A headless torso dress form on a pole stand serves as the projection surface. Its matte-white fabric body reflects projected colour faithfully and its familiar silhouette grounds the abstract brainwave visuals in fashion.

  • Headless torso dress form — no arms, matte white surface, pole stand base
  • Projector at 1.5–2.5 m — perpendicular to torso front, slightly elevated
  • Stoner COMP corner-pin — maps image exactly to shoulder-to-hip boundary
  • Darkened room — blackout curtains or dark exhibition booth for best colour saturation
  • Gaffer-taped base — prevents stand movement after calibration is set
